Alibaba Cloud Summit
Philippines 2024

2024年阿里云菲律宾峰会

Dragon AI produced the Alibaba Cloud Summit Philippines 2024 — a full-scale enterprise technology conference held at the Grand Hyatt Hotel in Manila. The event brought together hundreds of business leaders, IT decision-makers, and cloud professionals for a day of keynotes, product showcases, and networking sessions centered on digital transformation and cloud innovation.

From stage design and AV production to event logistics and on-site branding, Dragon AI delivered a seamless, high-impact summit experience that reinforced Alibaba Cloud's position as the leading cloud provider for Philippine enterprises.
